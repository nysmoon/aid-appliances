ABOUT US

Appliance Repair Services in Los Angeles

Since established, AID Appliances have over years of experience at the forefront of the appliance installations and maintenance industry, quickly becoming one of the biggest independent appliance repair companies in Los Angeles.

From our base in Los Angeles, we are ideally located to serve clients, providing the highest standard of diagnostic appliances servicing on all tumble dryers, washing machines, refrigerators, gas and electric cookers, extractor hoods, ovens, and much more.

We have built a reputation for offering the highest level of client care, and our professionals are all trained utilizing our bespoke system to keep them completely informed of all new products that become accessible.

AID Appliances provide the finest solution to all your appliance repair needs, and our professionals perform security checks as standard at every job.

We are fully committed to compressing our impact on the atmosphere as much as possible, and as such you can be certain that we do all we can to offer an eco-friendly service, including expelling all packaging and waste stuff from our visits and recycling all that we can!

AID Appliances are an authorized company for quality assurance. Do not hesitate to get in touch with our friendly and professional team today for more information.
